Case Brief

Client

Dave — lives and works in Singapore. Photographer of the disputed image.

Facts

Dave and his friend Tom visited the Mulu Caves in Sarawak on holiday, with a guide. Dave took numerous photographs on his phone before, inside, and outside the caves, to remember the trip and share with family and friends. He posted them to his Facebook page.

The dispute

Greg, Dave's friend, saw the photos on Facebook and submitted several of them — without telling Dave — to a Sarawak Tourism Board photo competition (RM5,000 prize). One of Dave's photos won. Greg collected the prize and was pictured in the newspapers receiving it.

What Dave wants

Public acknowledgment that it was his photograph, and the RM5,000 prize money.

Greg's position (disputed)
  • Dave cannot own rights in a photograph at all.
  • Even if he could, he gave up any rights by posting it on Facebook, where it was accessible to many people.
  • Dave's photo is probably much like Tom's from the same trip — no real skill or effort, "just snapping," one shot happened to turn out well.
